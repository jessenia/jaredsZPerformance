About Jared's Z Performance


Our Story

Jared's Z Performance was establised in 2012 by owner and operator, Jared Scarboro in Eatonton, GA but later moved his operations to Milledgeville, GA. What began with a passion for Nissan 300ZX's and to serve the strong community of Nissan enthusiasts, has flourished into an all around general service, repair, and towing shop for a variety of vehicle classes, engines, motors. At Jareds, we have a first in class service that focuses outward on our customer's needs.
